# FDV OPS 30-Day DePIN Article Calendar

This calendar contains 30 finished long-form X Article drafts, not post ideas.

## Structure

- 10 projects x 3 finished articles each
- Every day includes the complete article body
- Each article includes a title, 20-word preview, tagged intro under 250 characters, 5 subheadings, bullets, visual suggestion, CTA, source links, hashtags, and NFA
- Daily reminder time: 18:00 IST
- Posting to X remains manual
